## Core Features

### Subscription Plans & Billing
- Stripe-powered subscription billing — monthly, quarterly, annual
- Per-plan booking quotas (e.g. 4 sessions/month on Standard Plan)
- Plan-gated service access — customers can only book services covered by their plan
- One-off credit packs alongside subscriptions
- Customer self-service: pause, resume, and manage billing via Stripe portal

### Booking & Availability
- **Fixed slots** — pre-scheduled group classes with capacity limits (e.g. yoga class, 8 max)
- **Dynamic slots** — working-hours-based 1:1 appointments (generated from your availability)
- **Hybrid mode** — fixed group classes and dynamic individual sessions simultaneously
- Google Calendar integration — bookings appear as real calendar events; customers sync to personal calendar
- Multi-calendar support — separate calendars per staff member, location, or service area

### Website Builder
- Drag-and-drop page builder (no code required)
- Live commerce components — pricing plans and booking calendar embedded directly in your pages
- 13+ industry launch packs — launch a fully configured site in minutes
- Custom domain support with automatic SSL provisioning
- Per-page SEO settings, structured data (Schema.org JSON-LD), sitemap auto-generation

### Customer Portal
- Self-service portal: view subscriptions, remaining credits, upcoming and past bookings
- Cancel or reschedule bookings within configurable notice periods
- Stripe billing portal integration — customers manage payment methods and invoices themselves
- Support ticket system with knowledge base search

### WhatsApp AI Assistant
- LangGraph-powered conversational AI delivered over WhatsApp (Twilio)
- OTP authentication via WhatsApp — no separate login required
- Book, cancel, view bookings, and check account status via natural language
- Compound intent handling — "cancel Friday and book Monday at 6pm" in a single message
- Full conversation history logged to HubSpot contact timeline

### CRM & Integrations
- **HubSpot** — OAuth connect; auto-creates contacts on form submit; logs email, SMS, and WhatsApp to timeline
- **Google Analytics 4** — measurement ID injection, no code required
- **Google Business Profile** — connect and manage your listing from the admin panel
- **SendGrid** — transactional emails with custom domain sending (SPF/DKIM)
- **Twilio** — SMS notifications and WhatsApp AI channel
